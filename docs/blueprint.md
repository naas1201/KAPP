# **App Name**: Castillo Health & Aesthetics

## Core Features:

- Appointment Booking: Allow patients to easily book appointments online, with options for selecting service type, date, and time.
- Online Consultation Form: Provide a detailed form for new patients to fill out their medical history and aesthetic goals before their first consultation, including image upload and the ability to mark relevant information.
- Treatment Information Pages: Dedicated pages for each service offered (General Medicine, Aesthetic Treatments) with detailed information, pricing (where applicable), before & after photos, and FAQs. AI tool will be able to generate a FAQ automatically when a new treatment page is made
- Patient History Database: Securely store patient medical history, treatment records, and preferences for personalized care and easy access during appointments.
- Automated Reminders: Send automated SMS reminders to patients about upcoming appointments to reduce no-shows (optimized for Philippine networks).
- Lead Capture Form: A form that integrates directly into the patient database. Include integrations for other channels in the Philippine such as messenger.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A gentle, sophisticated purple (#B19CD9) that evokes feelings of trust and tranquility.
- Background color: A very light desaturated purple (#F5F3F7), providing a calm backdrop that focuses user attention on content.
- Accent color: A muted lavender (#D8B4FE), complementing the primary color, used for highlighting key elements and calls to action.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a modern, slightly warm, readable appearance suitable for body text.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif for headings to bring an elegant and fashionable feel; pairs well with 'PT Sans'.
- Use simple, line-based icons for easy navigation. Icons related to healthcare and beauty/wellness should be used.
- Clean, spacious layout with clear visual hierarchy for easy information access. Mobile-first responsive design.
